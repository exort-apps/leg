Agility is defined as the ability to move quickly and easily.
I would go even further and define it as the ability to be effortlessly nimble.
In order to achieve agility or enchance it, humans must periodically go through a regime of plyometric training plan.
Focusing on the sheer lower body power intertwined with impeccable core.
