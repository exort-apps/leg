Sally Up Challenge

Just 30 classic squats

Instructions

* Stand with your back straight and your feet shoulder width apart.
* Before you play the song lower yourself in the squat position.
* On every 'Sally Up' press yourself up and on every 'Sally down' get back in down squat position.
* On 'Old miss Lucy...' part of the song, stay in down squat position.
